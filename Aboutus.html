ABOUT BUCKETSTUDY

Our Vision

To be a global leader in task-based learning, empowering students and professionals with real-world skills and hands-on experience to thrive in their careers.

Our Mission

At BucketStudy, our mission is to create a practical learning ecosystem where students and professionals can gain industry-relevant skills through real-world tasks and internships. We aim to bridge the gap between education and employment by offering hands-on training that prepares learners for success in the job market.

Who We Are & What We Do

Founded in 2025, BucketStudy is a task-based internship platform designed to help students and professionals gain experience, upskill, and build their careers.

We provide real-world tasks and projects in high-demand fields, including:

  • Full Stack Development
  • Frontend Development (React, Angular, Vue, etc.)
  • Backend Development (Node.js, Django, Spring Boot, etc.)
  • UI/UX Design (Wireframing, Prototyping, Figma, Adobe XD)
  • Software Development
  • Data Science & AI
  • Cybersecurity & Blockchain
  • Cloud Computing & IoT
  • Digital Marketing & SEO
