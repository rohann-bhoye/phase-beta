Project Challenge

After registration, participants will receive a unique theme and will be tasked with creating a coding contest platform with the following features:

Implement secure login/signup functionality

Display available coding contests
Payment Integration

Real-time rankings of participants

Technical Requirements

  • Frontend: ReactJS (mandatory)
  • Backend: Firebase (mandatory) for database, authentication, and storage
  • Styling: TailwindCSS for all interfaces
  • AI Tools: Use of ChatGPT and other AI tools is permitted with transparency

No traditional backend required - Firebase will handle all your backend needs!

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the registration fee?
A nominal fee of ₹150 per team is required to ensure commitment and seriousness toward the project.
Can I participate individually?
Yes! You can participate either individually or as part of a team with up to 3 members.
Do I need prior experience with ReactJS?
While prior experience with ReactJS would be beneficial, this contest is also a great opportunity to learn. We recommend at least basic familiarity with JavaScript and React concepts.
How will the projects be evaluated?
Projects will be evaluated based on functionality, design, code quality, innovation, and how well they meet the requirements. The live presentation will also be a crucial part of the evaluation process.
When will the event dates be announced?
The exact contest dates will be shared with participants after registration. Make sure to register early to get ample time for preparation!
